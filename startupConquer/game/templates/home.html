GAME OVERVIEW

How to Play

Start your journey in one of four difficulty levels:
  • Easy: Southern states (Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh)
  • Medium: Western states (Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan)
  • Hard: Eastern states (Odisha, Bihar, West Bengal)
  • Very Hard: Northern states (UP, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh)
Each turn, spin the wheel to get a sector (e.g., Technology, Healthcare, Education). Your solution must match the sector assigned.
Each state presents a real-world issue unique to its people. Propose a creative startup idea that aligns with the sector to solve the problem. Your solution will be judged based on:
  • Sector Relevance: Does your idea match the chosen sector?
  • Problem Solving: How well does it tackle the issue?
  • Creativity and Feasibility: Is the idea innovative and practical?
Approved solutions claim the state for your team, marking it with your color on the map. Progress to the next state to continue expanding your domain.
Earn points for each successful solution and aim for a top rank. Speed and innovation can give you the edge needed to dominate the leaderboard!

WHY PLAY THIS GAME?

Test Your Skills: Sharpen your problem-solving abilities and creativity.
Learn and Innovate: Discover regional issues and brainstorm real-world solutions.
Compete Globally: Climb the leaderboard and challenge other players worldwide.
Fun Meets Purpose: Enjoy a rewarding gaming experience that combines education with entertainment.
