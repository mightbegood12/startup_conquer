About Startup Conqueror

Startup Conqueror is an interactive strategy game where creativity, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking come together. Dive into the diverse challenges faced by regions across India, propose innovative solutions, and expand your influence as you conquer state by state

Our Mission

Startup Conqueror aims to inspire players to think critically and creatively about real-world problems. By connecting regional issues with startup-driven solutions, we encourage innovation and foster an entrepreneurial mindset in a fun and engaging way.
